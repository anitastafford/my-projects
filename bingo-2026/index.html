How to play along

👍

Instructions:

  • When you complete a square, share a photo related to your completed task in the Signal group chat.
  • Anita will update this board to keep track of everyone's progress.
⭐

Achievements

  • Complete one row for kudos and awesomeness.
  • Complete both the diagonals (X) to show you're serious. (There may be prizes for all who completes this)
  • Blackout!! A prize and the coveted title of BINGO 2026 champion for the person who finishes first!
❓

Rules:

  • You cannot complete more than one square per day
  • No double daubing. One experience cannot complete more than one square
  • Though prizes are meant to motivate, the ideal is that participants will focus on the spirit of the activity instead of mere completion or competition. Be true to the intent of the square!
  • It's a-okay to get creative with your photos. If it's really not possible to take a photo related to your task, just send a message in the group chat.
